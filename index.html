Spreadsheet Entry

CSV Import

Teacher Support Needed

Use CSV import when students prepare items in a spreadsheet. The teacher should provide the template and explain which columns are required for the activity.

For a simple MC item, students usually need: topic, learningTarget, itemFormat, targetType, proposition, stem, optionA, optionB, optionC, optionD, answerKey, and distractorAnalysis.

For reasoning items, also include reasoningSkill, knowledgeNeeded, novelSituation, and reasoningPath.

App Notes

Privacy & Sources

Privacy

This app saves drafts locally in the browser on this computer. It does not need the internet for the current version.

Students should export DOCX/PDF copies for permanent submission. Clearing browser data may remove locally saved drafts.

Sources and Assessment Basis

The review prompts are informed by classroom assessment principles associated with Stiggins, Popham, Brookhart, McMillan, Nitko, and related assessment literature.

The app supports learning and revision. It does not replace teacher feedback, content validation, or instructor judgment.

Development Note

This app was developed by J. Arawiran for Assessment classes as a guided item-writing and item-review workspace.
